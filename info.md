Company Name: NexaTech Solutions

NexaTech Solutions is a technology company founded in 2020 that specializes in Internet of Things (IoT), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and embedded systems development. The company is headquartered in Lahore, Pakistan, and serves clients globally.

NexaTech provides services including smart home automation, industrial monitoring systems, and custom AI-powered dashboards. Their flagship product is an IoT-based environmental monitoring system that tracks temperature, humidity, and air quality in real time using MQTT protocol.

The company uses technologies such as Python, Flask, Node.js, and ESP32 microcontrollers. Their systems are designed to integrate with cloud platforms and provide real-time analytics through web dashboards.

NexaTech follows a structured workflow where incoming tasks are first stored in an "Inbox" system, then moved to "Needs_Action" for processing, and finally to "Done" after completion. This workflow is part of their internal AI employee system.

The company has a team of 15 engineers and focuses on innovation, automation, and scalable solutions. Their future goal is to expand into AI-driven robotics and smart city infrastructure.
